How it works
Community + Judges + Appeals
01
🥊
Issue a Beef
Challenge someone on any topic. Set your stake ($1–$500). Both parties must accept and deposit funds before the debate starts. No debate = full refund.
02
🎙️
Argue Your Case
Each debater gets 3 minutes to argue. Text, video, or both. The crowd watches live and places bets in real-time as odds shift.
03
⚖️
Multi-Layer Resolution
Winner is determined by: 60% community vote + 40% certified judge panel. For beefs over $200, a human moderator reviews the outcome before funds are released.
04
💸
Get Paid
Winner takes 95% of pot. Correct bettors share their side's pool proportionally. Instant withdrawal once verified. BEEF.gg takes 5%.

Common questions
What stops people from voting for their friend even if they lost?
Votes from accounts created within 7 days of the beef, or accounts with suspicious activity, are automatically excluded. The 40% judge component also provides an independent counterweight to crowd bias.
What if the result is genuinely ambiguous?
If the vote is within 52/48 margin, the beef is automatically escalated to senior judges. They can declare a draw — both debaters get their stake back, bettors are fully refunded.
Is this legal in the US?
BEEF.gg operates as a skill-based competition platform, not gambling. Winners are determined by argument quality and community judgment — not chance. We're operating in a similar legal framework to DraftKings and FanDuel.
What's the minimum bet to participate?
Debaters must stake a minimum of $1 to start a beef. Audience bettors can bet as little as $0.50. No maximum on bets, but large bets ($500+) require enhanced KYC verification.
